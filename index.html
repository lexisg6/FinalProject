Even though I don't know everything, as your new big sister the best thing I can do is share the things I have learned along the way in hopes that you can have a good foundation (and hopefully avoid learning these things the hard way as I did.) Even if you don't apply everything I advise, use these things as a guide to get started. I'll also provide any websites, books, or apps that I recommend. Good Luck!
